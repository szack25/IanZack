BIO

Photo by Sam Zack

Ian Zack has been a writer and editor for two decades. His work has appeared in the New York Times, Forbes, and many other publications. Zack's award-winning first book, Say No to the Devil: The Life and Musical Genius of Rev. Gary Davis, was called "magisterial" by the Wall Street Journal. He lives in New York City.
